The Scorecard As The Routing Hub

The scorecard sits at the centre of all routing. Every cold surface routes to the scorecard. The scorecard then routes the respondent per band into the right downstream:

  • Band 1 (Too Early) -> free YouTube playlist + entry book + light 5-email nurture
  • Band 2 (Ready for Apprentice-to-Master) -> cohort waitlist + Operator Track VSL + application opens on next cycle
  • Band 3 (Ready for Shop Owner) -> cohort waitlist + Operator Track VSL + application opens on next cycle
  • Band 4 (Too Far Along) -> podcast / community + opt-in for advisory work

The scorecard is the upstream router. Without the scorecard, every cold visitor lands on the same static page and gets the same downstream regardless of readiness.

Cross-Surface Repurpose (No New Content Load)

The existing YouTube and podcast cadence already provides the raw material for the welcome flow and the cohort nurture. Repurpose, do not re-shoot:

YouTube long-form -> welcome flow email

Pick the 10 highest-view tutorial videos from the last 24 months. Each becomes one email in a long-tail nurture series. Email body: 2-line context, embedded video, 1-line callback to the scorecard. Repurpose only, no re-shoot.

Podcast episode -> scorecard band routing

Tag the 30 most-listened episodes by band relevance (apprentice-relevant, shop-owner-relevant, broad). Each band's welcome flow gets the 5-7 most-relevant episodes embedded as a follow-up resource pack. Repurpose only.

IG clip -> social proof reel

The most-viewed IG clips become the social proof reel on the Operator Track landing (landing-page.html). Use the existing clips, not new shoots. The reel demonstrates Craig's voice on camera without requiring a new shoot.

Book content -> bonus chapter opt-in

A short bonus chapter (not in the print edition) becomes the lead magnet on acservicetech.com. Pulled from existing book content as a curated excerpt. Captures email above the podcast subscribe.

Post-Install Content Cadence (Ongoing, Optional)

After the install lands and the first cohort cycle runs, the optional content cadence additions are:

  • One YouTube tutorial per cohort cycle that calls back to the cohort theme (no sales pitch, just the relevant tutorial - Craig's standard register)
  • One podcast episode per cohort cycle that walks a real cohort scenario (anonymised, with permission)
  • One IG story sequence per cohort cycle showing the application surface (no sales pitch, just the surface)

These are optional. The four-piece install does not depend on them. The existing cadence carries the funnel.
