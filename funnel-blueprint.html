Architecture

AC Service Tech LLC | Funnel Blueprint

The current funnel, the target funnel, the install sequencing, and what sits where post-install. Architectural map for the four pieces in bottleneck-analysis.

Current Funnel (As Observable)

Cold YouTube watcher (500K-plus subs)
  -> link in description
  -> acservicetech.com static surface
       -> book sales link
       -> Amazon book listing
       -> one-time book purchase at book-tier pricing
       -> (Amazon-native post-purchase, no AG1-controllable touch)

Cold podcast listener (Apple Podcasts ID 1522359430)
  -> show note link
  -> acservicetech.com static surface
       -> (same routing as above)

Cold Instagram visitor (@acservicetech)
  -> link in bio
  -> acservicetech.com static surface
       -> (same routing as above)

Existing book buyer
  -> no segmented welcome flow
  -> no upgrade path beyond a second book purchase
  -> no cohort, no mid-tier, no community

What this funnel does:

  • Converts the audience into one-time book buyers at book-tier pricing
  • Routes 100 percent of free audience into the same static page with no readiness step
  • Does not capture email above the podcast subscribe
  • Does not segment the book buyer, podcast subscriber, or YouTube viewer
  • Does not surface a paid mid-tier above the books

What this funnel does NOT do (the gap):

  • Capture the email of a single Instagram visitor, YouTube viewer, or podcast listener without a book purchase first
  • Route the warmest book buyer into a higher-AOV next step
  • Filter cohort-ready prospects from book-tier-ready prospects before checkout
  • Build the in-house email asset that the cohort launch would mobilise

Target Funnel (Post-Install)

Cold YouTube watcher (500K-plus subs)
  -> link in description (routes to scorecard, not the static page)
  -> "Where Is Your HVAC Career In The Operator Ladder?" scorecard
       -> 90-second 12-question quiz
       -> band result + 3-line bottleneck readout
       -> email captured, tagged by band, years-on-tools, state, revenue band, track preference
       -> routed per band:

            Band 1 (Too Early)
              -> free YouTube playlist
              -> entry book on Amazon
              -> light nurture (5 emails over 30 days)

            Band 2 (Ready for Apprentice-to-Master Cohort)
              -> cohort waitlist landing
              -> Operator Track VSL (5-7 min, Craig's voice)
              -> book buyer bonus chapter (if not already purchased)
              -> application opens on next-cycle date

            Band 3 (Ready for Shop Owner Cohort)
              -> cohort waitlist landing (shop-owner variant)
              -> Operator Track VSL (5-7 min, Craig's voice)
              -> shop-owner case framing (no specific revenue claims, operator development frame)
              -> application opens on next-cycle date

            Band 4 (Too Far Along)
              -> podcast/community
              -> opt-in to be contacted for advisory work (light, no sales pressure)

Cold podcast listener (Apple Podcasts ID 1522359430)
  -> show note link (routes to scorecard or to the bonus chapter opt-in)
  -> same scorecard funnel as above OR
  -> bonus chapter opt-in -> book buyer welcome flow

Cold Instagram visitor (@acservicetech)
  -> link in bio (routes to scorecard, not the static page)
  -> same scorecard funnel as above

Existing book buyer (opted into the bonus chapter)
  -> Book Buyer welcome flow (Branch 1 of the segmented welcome)
  -> day 14 -> cohort introduction email
  -> day 30 -> Operator Track VSL embed link
  -> day 45 -> next-cycle cohort application invitation

Existing podcast subscriber (opted into the show-note download)
  -> Podcast Subscriber welcome flow (Branch 2)
  -> day 14 -> cohort introduction email
  -> day 30 -> Operator Track VSL embed link
  -> day 45 -> next-cycle cohort application invitation

Cohort applicant (Approved)
  -> enrolment link
  -> kickoff onboarding sequence

Cohort applicant (Not Approved This Cycle)
  -> next-cycle waitlist
  -> 6-week re-application nurture (operator-readiness training, journal cadence, scaffolding samples)

What this funnel does that the current one does not:

  • Captures email at scale across YouTube, podcast, and Instagram surfaces
  • Routes prospects by readiness band before they hit any buy surface
  • Surfaces a paid mid-tier (cohort) above the books
  • Segments the warmest existing buyer base (book buyers, podcast subscribers) into a sequence that ladders to the cohort
  • Routes cohort applicants who do not make this cycle into a next-cycle nurture rather than dropping them

The Four Installs Sequenced

The cohort offer architecture sits on top of the existing brand and the existing curriculum. No course rebuild. No re-platform. No brand pivot.

Install Sequence (Day-By-Day, 14-Day Plan)

Day What ships
Day 1 Voice calibration call (30-45 min). Brand colour confirm. Scorecard question set drafted. Cohort name and price band locked. Welcome-flow branch map.
Day 2 VSL scripts v1 drafted (homepage, book-tier, cohort-tier, all three from one record day). Scorecard logic and routing wired in dev environment. Cohort and waitlist landing pages wireframed.
Day 3 VSL record day (remote or in-person, single shoot covers all three cuts). Scorecard live on staging. Cohort landing copy v1.
Day 4 VSL editing in progress. Scorecard QA. Cohort landing copy v2. Welcome sequence Branch 1 (Book Buyer) drafted.
Day 5 VSL editing complete. Scorecard staging-to-production push. Welcome sequence Branch 2 (Podcast Subscriber) drafted. Cohort Typeform application wired.
Day 6 VSL embed live on acservicetech.com hero. Scorecard public on a unique URL. Welcome sequence Branch 3 (Scorecard Completer, forked per band) drafted.
Day 7 Operator Track cohort landing page live. Application Typeform live. Welcome sequence Branch 4 (Cohort Applicant) drafted.
Day 8 Email platform tagging logic live. ConvertKit (or equivalent) automations connected. Cross-branch routing rules tested end-to-end.
Day 9 Bonus chapter download surface live on acservicetech.com (the email-capture asset for the existing book buyer).
Day 10 YouTube description routing updated on the most recent 20 uploads (link to scorecard or bonus chapter, not the static page).
Day 11 Podcast show notes routing updated on the most recent 20 episodes (link to scorecard or bonus chapter).
Day 12 Instagram link in bio updated to the scorecard. Static page kept for legacy traffic.
Day 13 Full funnel walkthrough with Craig. Application review playbook handoff. Cohort first-cycle date locked.
Day 14 Kickoff campaign draft (existing list email + IG + YouTube community post + podcast trailer mention).

Craig's Required Time

  • Day 1: 30-45 min voice calibration call
  • Day 3: VSL record day (3-6 hours depending on single-cut vs. multi-cut record)
  • Day 13: 60-min full-funnel walkthrough
  • Day 14: 30-min kickoff campaign approval

Total: One half-day for the record, plus three short calls. The rest is parallel-built.

What Sits Where Post-Install

acservicetech.com (Wix-hosted)

  • Hero: VSL embed (5-7 min, Craig's operator-grounded register)
  • Below hero: scorecard CTA ("Where Is Your HVAC Career In The Operator Ladder? Find out in 90 seconds.")
  • Mid-page: book product line (existing, unchanged)
  • Footer: bonus chapter opt-in (the email capture above the podcast subscribe)
  • Operator Track cohort link in nav

Book Sales Surfaces (Amazon Listings + acservicetech.com book pages)

  • The Amazon listing is owned by Amazon (no embed possible inside the listing itself). The acservicetech.com book sales surface gets a 3-5 min book-tier VSL embed above the buy CTA.
  • Each book gets a bonus chapter opt-in CTA on the acservicetech.com surface (the email capture).

The Operator Track Cohort Landing

  • Hero: cohort-tier 5-7 min VSL (Craig's voice)
  • Who this is for: 3-card grid (Apprentice-to-Master applicant, Shop Owner applicant, Equivalent-Experience applicant)
  • The work: module breakdown for both cohort tracks
  • About Craig: NJ Master License, Licensed Teacher of HVACR / Sheet Metal / Building Maintenance, 16-plus years on tools, the 500K-plus YouTube channel, the three published books, the podcast cadence
  • FAQ: 8-10 cohort-specific questions
  • Apply: Typeform application embed
  • Investment-niche disclaimer footer (regulatory frame)

The Scorecard

  • 90-second 12-question quiz
  • 4 band result pages, each with the 3-line bottleneck readout
  • Email capture on completion
  • Routes per band per the Target Funnel diagram above

The Email Platform (ConvertKit Or Equivalent)

  • Tag schema: by band, years-on-tools, state, current-revenue-band (for shop-owner respondents), cohort-track-preference, buyer-type (book / podcast / scorecard / cohort-applicant)
  • 4 welcome flow branches per the target funnel above
  • Cross-branch suppression rules (cohort applicant gets paused on the Book Buyer Welcome to avoid sequence collision)
  • Re-application nurture for not-approved cohort applicants

The Existing Free Assets (Unchanged)

  • YouTube channel @acservicetechchannel - cadence unchanged, descriptions updated to route to the scorecard
  • Podcast "AC Service Tech Answers" - cadence unchanged, show notes updated to route to the scorecard or the bonus chapter
  • Instagram @acservicetech - cadence unchanged, link in bio updated

Regulatory Frame

Operator-development cohort. Not ticketed trade training. EPA 608, state licensure, and manufacturer-certified programs stay with the existing official providers. Results vary by market, capital, and operator execution.

The cohort never promises a license, never replaces an apprenticeship program, never substitutes for EPA 608, and never substitutes for manufacturer training. The cohort scaffolds operator development AROUND the official credential ladder.

What Stays The Same

  • Craig's voice, register, and on-camera presence
  • The YouTube channel
  • The podcast
  • The Instagram
  • The three books and the book-tier pricing
  • The acservicetech.com Wix-hosted infrastructure (the changes are inside the page surfaces, not a re-platform)

What Is New

  • The scorecard (the upstream router that did not exist)
  • The Operator Track cohort (the paid mid-tier that did not exist)
  • The 5-7 min homepage VSL, the 3-5 min book-tier VSL, and the 5-7 min cohort-tier VSL (the pre-sell asset that did not exist)
  • The segmented welcome flow (the in-house email asset that did not exist at scale)
  • The bonus chapter opt-in (the email capture surface that did not exist above the podcast subscribe)

The deeper read sits in bottleneck-analysis. The cohort surface sits in landing-page.html. The outreach copy sits in outreach.
